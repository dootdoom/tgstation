08 July 2020

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • The bounty board has been added to all standard NT stations, for making and paying out bounties for credits made by crew members across the station. Available as a standalone machine, as well as a modular app.

Fikou updated:

  • fixes labor camp beacon sprite
  • manly dorf now doesnt turn on dorf mode with alcohol tolerance

FloranOtten updated:

  • Space Cops now come equipped with appropriate environmental protection.

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • Fixed cryo cells locking up those with prosthetic limbs.
  • Fixes tend wounds not healing and brain surgery not curing traumas.

Melbert updated:

  • Mech vs Mech combat system for toy mechs! Hit a toy mech with another toy mech, or attack a player holding a toy mech with a toy mech, and you can initiate battle between toys!
  • Each toy mech now has its own health stat and special attack move for use in mech vs mech combat. Discover them all!
  • Suicide act for mech toys. If you wanted to take part in the toy mech battles yourself.

ShizCalev updated:

  • Added more examine text to bots.
  • Fixed player controlled statues being able to speak.
  • Fixed nonhuman that aren't able to speak being able to cast spoken spells
  • Fixed a minor runtime caused by removing a laser pointer's diode while it's still turned on.

Tlaltecuhtli updated:

  • votes now play a beep noise when they get started

Wayland-Smithy updated:

  • Blob mobs from an overmind now see critical mass progress as a status.

oranges updated:

  • Where some names might previously have been accepted, they will now be rejected, this should not affect any regular names in use

tralezab updated:

  • Removes the 9 assistants 3 changeling setup, yech.
  • Lotta bugfixes for mafia!
  • Mafia now alerts you when starting a game!
  • grammar + better text for some roles at better times of the game
  • LET THERE BE MAFIA DOCUMENTATION!
